Better Bats overhauls the vanilla Bat into a dynamic, ecosystem-driven entity. It turns the useless ambient creature into a swarming, light-sensitive, and symbiotic neighbor.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🌪️ Hive Mind (Social Swarms)
Bats no longer fly in chaotic, individual patterns. They organize into cohesive swarms using the **DasikLibrary** Hive Mind system. Witness groups of bats moving as one through the cave systems.

### 💩 Guano Fertility (Natural Growth)
Forget magical bonemeal. Bats roosting upside down slowly accumulate guano. Every 10 minutes, they fertilize the soil beneath them. If you have a roost above your farm, expect faster crop growth!

### 💡 Phototaxis (Light Sensitivity)
Just like moths, bats are attracted to bright artificial light. They will break from their swarms to circle lanterns and torches, hunting for insects drawn to the glow.

### 🛡️ Pest Control (Symbiotic Defense)
Bats are your allies against the small things that crawl. They will aggressively dive-bomb and one-shot **Silverfish** and **Endermites** in their territory.

### 📢 Acoustic Panic
Caves are quiet for a reason. Loud noises like explosions or nearby mining will wake resting swarms instantly, sending them into a frantic scatter.

---

## ⚙️ Configuration

Better Bats uses **GameRules** for real-time, server-side configuration. You can change these values in the world settings or via commands:

- `batSwarmSize`: Max bats allowed in a single flock (Default: 5).
- `batGuanoThreshold`: Ticks required to produce guano (Default: 12000). Lower is faster.
- `batPestControl`: Toggle Silverfish and Endermite hunting (Default: true).
